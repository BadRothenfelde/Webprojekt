Allergenes:

  1. Gluten
    • Found in soy sauce, tempura, noodles, dumplings, and breaded/fried items.
  2. Peanuts
    • Present in satay sauce, peanut oil, or dishes like Kung Pao chicken.
  3. Tree Nuts
    • Includes cashews, almonds, walnuts, and hazelnuts in sauces, toppings, or desserts.
  4. Shellfish
    • Includes shrimp, crab, lobster, shrimp paste, or fish roe.
  5. Fish
    • Found in fish sauce, anchovies, or dried fish flakes.
  6. Eggs
    • Used in fried rice, noodles, batter, or dishes like tamago sushi.
  7. Dairy
    • Includes cream, butter, or milk in desserts and fusion dishes.
  8. Soy
    • Found in tofu, soy milk, soy sauce, miso, or bean pastes.
  9. Sesame
    • Present in sesame oil, sesame seeds, or tahini.
  10. Sulfites
    • Found in dried fruits, wine, vinegar, and marinades.
  11. Lupin
    • Occasionally in noodles or pastries.
  12. Mustard
    • Found in Indian dishes, dressings, or mustard oil.
  13. Celery
    • Common in soups, stocks, or seasoning mixes.
  14. Mollusks
    • Includes squid, clams, mussels, and oyster sauce.
  15. Alcohol
    • Found in cooking wine (e.g., Shaoxing, mirin) or desserts.
  16. Nightshades
    • Includes chili peppers, paprika, eggplant, and tomatoes.
  17. Citrus
    • Found in lemongrass, lime, yuzu, or sauces.
  18. Seeds
    • Includes poppy seeds or pumpkin seeds in desserts or garnishes.
